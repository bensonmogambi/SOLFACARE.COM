About Us

Wherever you are, SolfaCare, a mobile provider of intravenous therapy, will deliver IV treatments right to your door. As well as onsite employee checkups and testing for businesses and corporations, as well as child vaccination, we now offer in-home treatment, checkups, and testing for individuals and families. All procedures are carried out by registered nurses.

Our Mission

We are committed to healing the unseen because not all wounds are visible. We accomplish this by assisting our patients in overcoming mental health challenges. We are committed to keeping our communications and treatment private, sympathetic, and approachable.

Vision

Exceeding expectations by being available when needed. Supporting diagnostic confidence in order to keep the prices of our high-quality services affordable for end users. Acting in unison, with sincerity, a pioneering spirit, and collaboration. Assuming social responsibility and environmental protection through eco-friendly products and services.

Our Services

We provide all medical treatments and specialize in IV Therapy, Iron Infusion, and Pediatric Immunization.

VITAMIN IV THERAPY.

For wellness and recovery, we offer IV treatments containing fluids, vitamins, and antioxidants.

IRON INFUSION.

We offer patients at-home iron infusion therapy to effectively help them return to normal iron levels.

IV NAD THERAPY.

For the purpose of promoting youth, energy, healthy brain function, and addiction recovery, we provide NAD IV therapy.

I.V. KETAMINE THERAPY.

We offer ketamine IV treatments for addiction and mental health conditions like depression, anxiety, and post-traumatic disorder (PTSD).
